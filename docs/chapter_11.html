7.2 Adjuvant therapies for diabetes

In this example, we consider the synthesis of the following data:

  • Aggregate data from 35 randomized trials investigating the effectiveness of adjuvant therapies for diabetic patients, and
  • Individual participant data from a prospective cohort study investigating patient and limb survival in patients with diabetic foot ulcers

We first retrieve the randomized evidence.

library(dplyr)
library(jarbes)

data("healing")
AD <- healing %>% dplyr::select(y_c, n_c, y_t, n_t, Study)
names(AD) <- c("yc", "nc", "yt", "nt", "Study")
